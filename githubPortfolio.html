Dune: Special Spice Ops

Dune: SSO is a RTT game developed in 4 months by 23 students for the subject "Project 3" in our degree. As part of the design team I and my colleagues worked on the documentation, character design, level design and mechanics of the game with the given prompts. My most important tasks were my contributions to the characater design of all used and discarded heros, as well as the implementation of the levels and lighting and feedback cues studies. See what I did!
The game's website

PaintWars

Paint Wars is an isometric single player RTS game featuring a campaign with playable units, buildable structures and manageable resources where the player will have to fight against waves of enemies trying to destroy his base. The game is located in a paint themed world where resources, units and buildings will be related to paint.

Ninja Frog Against Gravity

Ninja Frog Against Gravity is a platformer where your goal is to find the way out. The player is a Ninja Frog, who has to find the exit falling through holes where the gravity changes and walk downwards. It's a platformer with two levels and a small tutorial, where the player can move right and left, jump and double jump.
